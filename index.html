How to find your ID

1

Log in to the official fantasy.premierleague.com website.

2

Click on the "Points" tab in the main navigation menu.

3

Look at the URL in your browser address bar. It will look like this:

.../entry/123456/event/...
4

The number in the middle (e.g., 123456) is your Team ID.
